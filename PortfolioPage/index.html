With this portfolio, I aim to provide an insight into my skills, projects, and expertise that I have acquired during my master’s program in “Human-Computer Interaction” at Paris Lodron University and the University of Applied Sciences Salzburg. In Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), we develop ideas or improvements for interacting with technology — from user navigation on websites to the control of machines. We explore how we can use technology to address real-world problems by developing design concepts, implementing prototypes, and subsequently evaluating them. Throughout this process, we follow a user-centered approach and utilize various research and design methods to find suitable solutions.
